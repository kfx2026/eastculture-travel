About Oriental Cities Travel

Discover the beauty of China's cities through the eyes of locals.

Who We Are

Oriental Cities Travel is a curated travel guide to China's most fascinating urban destinations. We go beyond tourist hotspots to uncover the authentic character of each city — from thousand-year-old teahouses to cutting-edge art districts.

Our Mission

Travel should inform, not just entertain. Our guides combine historical context, cultural insights, and practical tips to help travelers experience Chinese cities with depth and respect. We prioritize local voices and off-the-beaten-path discoveries.

Editorial Standards

City guides are written and fact-checked by our editorial team. We draw from academic sources, local expert interviews, UNESCO heritage documentation, and firsthand travel experience. Guides are updated regularly to reflect current conditions.
